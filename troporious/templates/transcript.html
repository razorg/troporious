Instructions

This service is intended for people with disabilities. You can now call every person you want, and communicate in a respected way. By combining many technological means, the other end can - like always - listen what you type in this web application by utilizing computer voice. Only thing you have to do after receiving the call, is typing what you want to say. When the called person responds, what he said, will be shown in this page, you can read it and the conversation will go on. Appropriate instructions for the called person will be said on the phone.
